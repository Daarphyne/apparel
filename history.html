History of Innovation

The Shakarzahi Micro power Constant Electric Energy System device is an invention of Shakarzahi LLC. The innovation arose due to the need for an alternative renewable energy source that is reliable, effective, sustainable, cost-friendly, and of course, no emissions. The invention is the work of several scientists who have worked tirelessly to develop an innovation that meets energy demand and is a highly effective, environmentally friendly, cost-saving, and renewable energy source.

SHAKARZAHI LLC understood perfectly where the energy industry is lacking, which brings us to the invention of The SHAKARZAHI constant electric energy power system device. The innovation is targeted towards eliminating the challenges the United States and every other country are facing around the world regarding electric energy generation. Having a device that assures constant energy distribution, elimination of greenhouse gases, and affordable and readily available energy is the breakthrough the energy sector needs to end the ongoing electricity crises and outages.
